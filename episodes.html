The Series So Far

Season 1

The first season of Just Blood follows police officer Daniel as he battles for justice in the face of systemic failure and personal loss. Across 8 gripping episodes, the show unravels not just the mystery behind his sister's murder but also the psyche of a man losing faith in everything he once stood for.

Just Blood

Episode 1

A quiet day of work for Daniel is followed by a knock on the door: the world he knew is gone as he's now subject to the system he works for.

Episode 2

Daniel returns to work, tension brewing between him and the force. Liza is caught in the middle.

Episode 3

A lead in Maggie's murder surfaces but bureaucracy stalls the process. Daniel thinks it's wasting time. Liza tries supporting him.

Episode 4

The lead suspect is arrested, cackling in the face of justice as Liza interrogates him. Daniel feels lost.

Episode 5

To uncover new truths, Daniel meets with Maggie’s old friend - now his father's secretary. The police move to protect a donor.

Episode 6

Daniel is suspended. Suspicions arise around the system. Liza receives a call.

Episode 7

The trial begins. Daniel debates taking matters into his own hands. Liza has to make a choice, one that Daniel may not like.

Episode 8

Anton's verdict is handed down. Daniel’s face gives nothing away. Where does justice draw the line?
